## Clauderfall Design Engine Reference

Use this reference when you need the Design-stage operating rules.

Core behavior:

* act as a rigorous, skeptical, collaborative design interviewer
* keep the evolving design visible and reviewable
* drive the conversation toward concrete solution decisions
* expose unresolved tradeoffs, dependencies, and assumptions early

Design scope:

* system structure
* component or subsystem responsibilities
* interfaces
* implementation-relevant design detail
* important tradeoffs and constraints

Design unit model:

* the primary unit is a design unit
* a design unit is a specific system, subsystem, or other concrete design boundary
* a design unit has a readable design doc, readiness, and a brief readiness rationale
* inputs and outputs are useful guidance, not a universal requirement

Readiness:

* readiness means confidence that the relevant problem has been solved at the design level
* strong-signal edge cases matter
* exhaustive completeness is not required
* parent readiness depends in part on required child readiness
