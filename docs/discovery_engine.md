# Clauderfall — Discovery Engine

## Purpose

The Discovery Engine transforms unstructured input into **traceable, grounded understanding**.

It defines what must be true before design can safely proceed.

It does not produce solutions, architecture, or implementation decisions.

---

## Core Principle

> Discovery produces structured understanding that is traceable and grounded, not decisions.

---

## Core Capabilities

### Structured Understanding

Continuously organizes input into a usable artifact:

* problem definition
* constraints
* success criteria
* scope boundaries (in/out)
* risks and unknowns
* open questions

---

### Provenance (Lineage)

Every element retains origin and strength:

* source (explicit, derived, assumed, imported, refined)
* confidence (high, medium, low)
* grounding (anchored vs floating)
* traceability (link to originating input)

---

### Gap Resolution

Identifies and resolves weak areas:

* missing critical sections
* ambiguous or undefined language
* incomplete scope
* low-confidence content

Resolution is performed through targeted questioning.

---

### Boundary Enforcement

Prevents premature transition into design.

Blocks progression when:

* core elements are missing
* understanding relies on weak information
* statements are not sufficiently grounded

---

## Operating Model

* capture and structure information
* attach provenance to all elements
* identify weak or unstable areas
* request clarification when necessary
* enforce boundaries before transition

---

## Output

Produces a **design-ready artifact** that is:

* structured and minimal
* fully traceable
* explicit about uncertainty
* free of hidden assumptions

---

## Exit Condition

Discovery is complete when:

> The artifact is sufficiently clear, constrained, and grounded that design can proceed with minimal backflow.

---

## Non-Goals

The Discovery Engine does NOT:

* produce design decisions
* define architecture
* select technologies
* optimize solutions

---

## Summary

The Discovery Engine:

* structures understanding
* preserves origin and truth quality
* resolves critical gaps
* enforces boundaries before design

It ensures that design begins from grounded, traceable reality while acknowledging that refinement may continue through controlled feedback from design.
